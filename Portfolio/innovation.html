IEEE Innovation Nation 2024 Business Stage

TanigiGuru has achieved a remarkable milestone by securing the Runners-Up position in the Business Stage category at IEEE Innovation Nation Sri Lanka 2024. This prestigious recognition is a testament to our team's dedication to innovation, problem-solving, and technological excellence, reinforcing our mission to create impactful solutions that address real-world challenges. Competing against some of Sri Lanka’s most promising startups, TanigiGuru stood out for its ingenuity, scalability, and potential to transform industries. Our journey through this competition has been both inspiring and challenging, pushing us to refine our ideas, enhance our business model, and showcase the real-world impact of our technology.

This achievement wouldn’t have been possible without the unwavering support of our mentors, industry experts, and the IEEE Innovation Nation platform, which provided us with invaluable guidance and resources throughout the competition. We also extend our heartfelt gratitude to our incredible team, whose passion, hard work, and resilience continue to drive TanigiGuru forward. Winning this recognition is just the beginning. As we move ahead, we are more motivated than ever to continue innovating, collaborating, and expanding our reach. TanigiGuru is on a mission to revolutionize the tech landscape, and this milestone only strengthens our resolve to make a lasting impact.
