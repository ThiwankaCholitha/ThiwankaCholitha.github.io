Microsoft Imagine Cup

TangiGuru, a team from Sri Lanka, achieved notable success in the 2023 Microsoft Imagine Cup, a global competition that encourages student developers to create innovative technology solutions addressing real-world challenges. Competing in the Education category, TangiGuru developed a project aimed at enhancing early childhood education for children with learning disabilities. Their solution focused on fostering critical thinking and promoting independent learning, thereby supporting both physical and mental development.
TECHCOMMUNITY.MICROSOFT.COM

  • Epic Challenge Runners-Up
  • SEA/NM Finals Runners-Up

In the Southeast Asia New Markets Regional Competition, TangiGuru was recognized as a runner-up, earning a cash prize of USD $500 and access to mentorship to refine their project further. This recognition underscores the team's commitment to leveraging technology for educational advancement and their potential to make a significant impact in the field.
NEWS.MICROSOFT.COM

The Imagine Cup's emphasis on innovation and social impact provided TangiGuru with a platform to showcase their dedication to improving education for children with learning disabilities. Their achievements in the competition highlight the growing role of technology in addressing educational challenges and the importance of supporting initiatives that promote inclusive learning environments.
